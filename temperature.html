Max Temperature vs. Latitude

A negative latitude represents a region in Southern Hemisphere and a positive latitude represents a region in the Northern hemisphere. Climate differs between Northern and Southern Hemispheres due to seasonal slant towards and away from the sun. Hemisphere with the slant towards the sun experiences higher temperatures than the Hemisphere with the slant away from the sun.
Above plot shows higher maximum temperatures in the Southern Hemisphere than that in the Northern Hemisphere at the time the data was gathered, namely, Dec.20,2017.
